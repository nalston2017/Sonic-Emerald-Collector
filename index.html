Emerald Game

Here's how you play the game

There are 4 rings, each has a random number associated with them. By clicking on a ring, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. If your total score matches the target score you win and you have helped Sonic get an emerald. You lose if your total score goes above the random number. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each ring.
